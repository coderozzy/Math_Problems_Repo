# Task 8 â€” Joint Distribution of Independent Variables

## Given

- $P(X=1) = 0.6$, $P(X=2) = 0.4$
- $P(Y=1) = 0.5$, $P(Y=2) = 0.5$
- $X$ and $Y$ are independent

## Solution

Since $X \perp Y$: $P(X=i, Y=j) = P(X=i) \cdot P(Y=j)$

| $Y \backslash X$ | 1 | 2 | $P(Y)$ |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | $0.6 \times 0.5 = 0.30$ | $0.4 \times 0.5 = 0.20$ | 0.50 |
| **2** | $0.6 \times 0.5 = 0.30$ | $0.4 \times 0.5 = 0.20$ | 0.50 |
| $P(X)$ | 0.60 | 0.40 | 1.00 |
