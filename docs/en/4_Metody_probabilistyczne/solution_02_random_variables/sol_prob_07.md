## Given Joint Distribution

| $Y \backslash X$ | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **2** | 0.1 | 0.2 | 0.3 |
| **4** | 0.1 | 0.1 | 0.2 |

## Marginal Distribution of Y

$$P(Y=2) = 0.1 + 0.2 + 0.3 = 0.6$$

$$P(Y=4) = 0.1 + 0.1 + 0.2 = 0.4$$

## CDF of Y

$$\boxed{F_Y(y) = \begin{cases}0 & y < 2\\ 0.6 & 2 \le y < 4\\ 1.0 & y \ge 4\end{cases}}$$
