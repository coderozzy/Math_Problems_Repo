# Task 9 â€” Finding Constant c for Joint Density

## Given

$$f(x,y) = \begin{cases}cxy & 0 \le x \le 2,\; 0 \le y \le 1\\ 0 & \text{otherwise}\end{cases}$$

## Condition

The total integral must equal 1:

$$\int_0^2 \int_0^1 cxy\,dy\,dx = 1$$

$$c \int_0^2 x\,dx \cdot \int_0^1 y\,dy = 1$$

$$c \cdot \left[\frac{x^2}{2}\right]_0^2 \cdot \left[\frac{y^2}{2}\right]_0^1 = 1$$

$$c \cdot 2 \cdot \frac{1}{2} = 1$$

$$c \cdot 1 = 1$$

$$\boxed{c = 1}$$
