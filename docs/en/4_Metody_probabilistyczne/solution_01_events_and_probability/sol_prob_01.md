## Given

$\Omega = \{\omega_1, \omega_2, \omega_3, \omega_4, \omega_5\}$, $A = \{\omega_1, \omega_3, \omega_5\}$, $B = \{\omega_2, \omega_3, \omega_4\}$

## Solutions

**1.** $A \cup B = \{\omega_1, \omega_2, \omega_3, \omega_4, \omega_5\} = \Omega$

**2.** $A \cap B = \{\omega_3\}$

**3.** $B \setminus A = \{\omega_2, \omega_4\}$ (elements in $B$ but not in $A$)

**4.** $A \setminus B = \{\omega_1, \omega_5\}$ (elements in $A$ but not in $B$)
